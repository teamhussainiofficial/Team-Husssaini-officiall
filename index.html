Our Mission

Hamari tanzeem ka asal maqsad hai:
Huzoor صلى الله عليه وسلم ki namoos ki hifazat karna,
aur jo log Allah Paak ya Huzoor صلى الله عليه وسلم ki shaan mein gustakhi karte hain,
unke against legal aur shar‘i tor par action lena.

Hum Mazloomon ke saath hain, unki madad karte hain, aur Madadgaron ko support karte hain jo insaaf aur aman ke liye kaam karte hain.
  • Huzoor صلى الله عليه وسلم ki namoos ki hifazat karna.
  • Gustakhi karne walon ke khilaaf legal aur shar‘i action lena.
  • Mazloomon aur madadgaron ki madad karna, insaaf aur aman ko barqarar rakhna.

“We do not endorse violence. All actions are lawful, peaceful, and follow shar‘i and legal guidance.”
