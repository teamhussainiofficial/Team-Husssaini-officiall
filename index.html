Our Mission

Huzoor صلى الله عليه وسلم ki namoos ki hifazat karna, aur jo log Allah Paak ya Huzoor صلى الله عليه وسلم ki shaan mein gustakhi karte hain unke khilaaf shar‘i aur legal tor par action lena. Hum Mazloomon ke saath hain, unki madad karte hain, aur Madadgaron ko support karte hain jo insaaf aur aman ke liye kaam karte hain.

We do not endorse violence. All actions are lawful, peaceful, and follow shar‘i and legal guidance.
